A personal invitation

Dear Alumni,

There is something remarkable about returning to the place where so many of your most important journeys began. The corridors where ideas sparked into life, the classrooms where challenges became opportunities, and the friendships that have lasted far beyond graduation — they are all still here, waiting for you.

We are overjoyed to invite you back to Fachhochschule Südwestfalen for our Alumni Reunion — an occasion dedicated entirely to you. Whether you graduated recently or many years ago, whether you have stayed close by or travelled from another city, your story is part of ours.

This is a moment to reconnect with peers who shared your lectures and study sessions, to meet the students who walk those same halls today, and to reflect on how far you have all come. We have prepared an evening full of warmth, conversation, and genuine celebration.

We have always believed that the true measure of a university is not just in the degrees it confers, but in the lives its graduates go on to lead. Looking at all of you — the innovators, the leaders, the communicators — we could not be more proud.

Please join us. Your place is ready, and we cannot wait to hear your story.

S

Mr. Stevenson

Communication Management · IMIS
